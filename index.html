About College Resource Hub

Welcome to College Resource Hub, an elite PWA designed to give students structural access to essential course materials without any stress.

Key Features:

  • Handwritten notes sorted chapter-wise.
  • Latest college syllabus documents (2025-26).
  • Syllabus matching past year question papers (PYQs).
  • Full offline capability with instant caching pipelines.

This platform is crafted to deliver structural accessibility to students completely for free.
